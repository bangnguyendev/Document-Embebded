Resizing and removing hyperlinks and highlight areas

After you create a hyperlink or highlight, you can change the size of it or remove it completely.

It is easiest to resize and remove hyperlinks and highlights if you are working in Edit mode, rather than Preview mode. When you edit a document, selection handles appear around all hyperlinks and highlighted areas. These handles are rectangular, regardless of the shape of the hyperlink or highlight.

 

Before you resize or remove a highlight or hyperlink, make sure DjVu Solo is in the editing mode by clicking the Edit mode button on the toolbar.

To resize a hyperlink or highlight:

  • Place the cursor over a selection handle, so that it changes from a pointer to a double-ended arrow, and drag the handle.


     
    To resize the area proportionately, place the pointer over a corner handle; to change only the length or width, place it over a side handle.

To remove a hyperlink or highlight:

  • Right-click inside a hyperlink or highlight, and then choose Hyperlinks > Delete.
