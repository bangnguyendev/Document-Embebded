About DjVu Solo

DjVu® Solo 3.1 (Non-Commercial version) is the ideal tool for the individual user who needs to convert collections of paper documents into an electronic format. DjVu Solo delivers a powerful solution for creating high-quality documents with remarkably small file sizes. Now you can create compact "Image + Text" files quickly and easily, saving you time, storage space, and the costs associated with distributing and storing electronic files.

LizardTech provides DjVu Solo 3.1 Non-Commercial as a complimentary application for users to create robust DjVu documents for non-commercial purposes.  For information about commercial licensing of DjVu encoding products, visit www.lizardtech.com.

For more information, see About DjVu technology.

What’s new in DjVu Solo 3.1

DjVu Solo, formerly DjVu Shop, includes the following features:

  • Two new multiple-page file formats: indirect and bundled, which replace the old_indexed and old_bundled formats

  • Support for embedded thumbnails and shared dictionaries

  • Features for adding hyperlinks and highlighted areas to images

About LizardTech

Founded in 1992, LizardTech is a privately held company focused on developing innovative imaging solutions that provide users of all levels with bandwidth optimization and instant access to high-resolution, multipurpose digital images and multimedia content. The company licensed Generation I of its MrSID® technology from Los Alamos National Laboratory and acquired its DjVu technology from AT&T Labs for the commercialization of both product lines. The newly released LizardTech Content Server is the fastest Internet application for delivering high-resolution photographs and perfect reproductions of documents. LizardTech is now integrating its next-generation image software into a wide variety of platforms and applications.
