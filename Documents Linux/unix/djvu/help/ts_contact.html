Contacting LizardTech Technical Support

Gathering information

When you contact LizardTech Technical Support, please be ready with the following information to assist us in resolving the issue.

  • Other LizardTech products you have installed.

  • Which operating system you use.

  • How much free hard drive space your computer has.

  • How much RAM your computer has.

  • The task you were working on when the problem occurred.

  • The exact error message, if applicable.

  • Whether you can reproduce the problem.

  • Any other information you feel is relevant to the issue.
